Everything you need to know about Okoa Sem

üí≥ How do I make payments? +
Payments are made through M-Pesa STK Push. Simply select your preferred plan (Daily at KSh 10 or Monthly at KSh 100), enter your M-Pesa phone number, and you'll receive a prompt on your phone to complete the payment. The process is instant and secure!
üìö How many past papers are available? +
We have over 24,000+ past papers from 8 different schools covering 50+ departments. Papers are organized by year, semester, and course code, making it easy to find exactly what you need for your exam preparation.
ü§ñ What can the AI Study Bot do? +
Our AI Study Bot provides instant answers to your academic questions, helps explain complex concepts, generates practice questions from your notes, and offers personalized study guidance. It's like having a personal tutor available 24/7!
üì• Can I download papers for offline use? +
Yes! With a premium subscription, you can download unlimited PDFs and access them offline anytime. This is perfect for studying when you don't have internet access or want to save on data.
